# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a respectful experience for everyone, regardless of background or experience level.

## Our standards

Expected behavior:
- Be respectful and constructive in discussions
- Accept feedback graciously
- Focus on what is best for the project

Unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment or personal attacks
- Dismissive or demeaning comments
- Sustained disruption of discussions

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the maintainer via GitHub. All reports will be reviewed and addressed. The maintainer reserves the right to remove comments or ban participants who violate this code of conduct.
